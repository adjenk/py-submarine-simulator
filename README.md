# 2D Submarine Simulator

## Features
- Working physics loop
- Propeller linear thrust and reverse
- Rigid body linear drag
- Rotational physics (moment of inertia, angular velocity, angular drag)
- Scrolling camera following the submarine
- Real GEBCO bathymetry data (Dover Strait)

## Todo
- Top-down view (hull, conning tower redrawn; propeller removed)
- Flat buoyancy (Archimedes principle, blocked until unit system resolved)
- Gradient water pressure buoyancy as extension of flat buoyancy
- Ocean currents affecting submarine velocity
- HUD (depth, speed, heading)
- First-person viewport as secondary view

## Stretch Goals
- Curved surface friction adjustment (aerodynamic nose cone drag dampening)
- Sea creatures
- Wrecks and manmade structures
- Weather effects

## What this will NOT simulate
- particle motion or pressure due to particle motion
- water dynamics: dynamic water column heights, water height due to submarine displacement
- Elastic bodies or deformation
- anything 3D
